Teriyaki Chicken

Description

A classic dish that's quick to make, featuring tender chicken glazed in a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken thighs
  • Sugar
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Pan-fry chicken until cooked through.
  2. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar, then pour over the chicken.
  3. Simmer until the sauce thickens to a glossy glaze.
