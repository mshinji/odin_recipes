Teriyaki Chicken

Description

A comforting soup that comes together in minutes, often served as a starter or breakfast in Japan.

Ingredients

  • Dashi stock
  • Miso paste
  • Silken tofu
  • Wakame seaweed
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Heat dashi stock in a pot (do not boil).
  2. Dissolve miso paste into the broth.
  3. Add tofu cubes and wakame, then warm through.
  4. Garnish with sliced green onions before serving.
